Resume in RMarkdown

Posted at — Mar 31, 2020

One of my professional goals this year is to use blogging as a medium to practice skills related to data science. As a first step, I have made my resume using pagedown. Thanks to Nick Strayer and his blogpost, I could create my resume in RStudio. The blog post was detailed and simplified the process for me. I added the Skills section in the Sidebar and used the Font Awesome icons. Once I had the various sections of my resume compiled in the spreadsheet as explained in the blogpost, I tweaked the CSS files for some formatting changes and had the resume saved as a PDF.
